When To Call

Signs you need Water Damage Restoration in San Marco

Recognizing the signs of water damage is crucial for homeowners in San Marco. Timely intervention can prevent further issues and costly repairs. Here are some common indicators that you may need our services.

1. Visible water stains on walls

These stains indicate that water has penetrated the walls, which can lead to structural damage if not addressed immediately.

2. Musty odors in your home

A musty smell often signals mold growth, which can be harmful to health and requires prompt remediation.

3. Peeling paint or wallpaper

Peeling or bubbling paint suggests moisture buildup behind the surfaces, indicating potential water damage.

4. Warped or buckling floors

Floors that are warped or buckling are signs of significant water damage, requiring immediate attention to prevent further issues.

5. Increased utility bills

A sudden spike in water bills may indicate a leak or other water damage issue that needs to be investigated.
