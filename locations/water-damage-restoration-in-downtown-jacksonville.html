When To Call

Signs you need Water Damage Restoration

Recognizing the signs of water damage early can save you from costly repairs in Downtown Jacksonville. If you notice any of the following symptoms, it's crucial to act quickly and call for professional help.

1. Visible water stains on walls

Water stains indicate a leak or moisture problem that needs immediate attention to prevent further damage.

2. Mold growth in corners

Mold thrives in damp conditions, and its presence is a clear sign of water damage that requires remediation.

3. Musty odors in your home

A musty smell often indicates hidden water damage or mold growth, signaling the need for a thorough inspection.

4. Peeling paint or wallpaper

When paint or wallpaper starts peeling, it's a sign that moisture is trapped underneath, indicating potential water damage.

5. Warped or buckling floors

Flooring that is warped or buckling can be a sign of water damage, requiring immediate restoration services.
